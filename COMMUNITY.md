## 2. Governance with BRAG
The ecosystem is powered by the **BRAG Token**, which allows holders to shape the future of the project.

*   **Earning BRAG:** You automatically receive 1,000,000 BRAG tokens for every $1 USD donated when minting a BragNFT.
*   **Voting:** Use your BRAG balance to vote on governance proposals, such as:
    *   Allocation of treasury funds to specific STEM programs.
    *   Approval of new "Verified Locations" in the `ExhibitRegistry`.
    *   Updates to protocol parameters (e.g., royalty percentages).
*   **Proposals:** Any holder with a significant stake can submit a proposal through our Snapshot or On-chain governance portal.

## 3. Contributing
We welcome contributions from developers, artists, and educators!

### Developers
*   Check the [Issues](https://github.com/digitaledsafety/smart-contracts/issues) tab for "Good First Issue" labels.
*   Review our `README.md` for local environment setup instructions.
*   Follow the code style and testing requirements (Hardhat + viem).

### Educators & Server Owners
*   Interested in integrating BragNFTs into your Minecraft server? Check out our `ExhibitVault` documentation in `FEATURES.md`.
*   Contact us on Discord for partnership opportunities and API access.

## 4. Security
Please report any security vulnerabilities to `security@brag.charity`. See our `SECURITY.md` for our full disclosure policy.
